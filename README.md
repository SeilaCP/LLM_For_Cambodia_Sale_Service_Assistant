# LLM_For_Cambodia_Sale_Service_Assistant (On Developing)
## Project Introduction
Problem :  micro business Cambodia isn’t have time to reply customer and hard to invest and find the sale assistant for their small business
Solution : Create sale service assistant to help reply customer chat and notify the customer churn
Objective : The sale service assistant intelligent for all Cambodia micro business models

Project Overview 

This Tools was aim to help the mini-shop or micro-sale online to communicate with their customer more easily and minimax their time as much as possible to support loner owner shop running their small business. 

Project Objective

- **Enable Online Sale Service Assistant:** Access Bot: this bot will aim to reply customer that was request or provide question of products. It will support the general question on our client products with in Khmer language.
- **Enable Product Controls Stock**: For this user need to provide the information of their products by category of their products for our models to understanding and their stock and price to follow up with their customer.

Project Scope
In-Scope:

- This models will aim to work with skincare products
- apply for telegram bot as default
- Simple dashboard that will save the chart history and analysis customer behavior and stoke tracking
